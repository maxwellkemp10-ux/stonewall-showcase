Control plane

  • Source OneDrive PDFs, DOCX, XLSX, screenshots, and Outlook CSV exports.
  • Ingestion ingest_onedrive.py and parse_emails.ps1 shape upstream files.
  • Processing email_consolidator.mjs, docx_to_md.py, and email_defuzz.mjs normalize the corpus.
  • Sync notion_wire_cases.py, notion_case_dates.py, and notion_consolidate_emails.py wire the case registry.
  • QC and reporting qc_sweep.mjs, verify_repo_consistency.py, and tactical_brief.py keep the surface auditable.

Built for the firehose.

Stonewall was battle-tested inside a high-volume insurance defense vertical. The point is not novelty theater. The point is to turn email, filings, depositions, and case events into a searchable, auditable intelligence layer that can actually survive contact with the work.

Ingest

Pull in Outlook exports and OneDrive documents without losing provenance, dates, or matter linkage.

Normalize

Deduplicate, convert, classify, and cross-link the material so it can be searched and trusted.

Audit

Run consistency sweeps and tactical reporting so the corpus keeps telling the truth when the organization does not.

Architecture in layers.

The repo's architecture note is straightforward: source files arrive, the processing layer converts them into structured text, Notion carries the relationships, AI fills the gaps, and QC closes the loop.

01 Source
OneDrive and Outlook feed the system.

PDFs, DOCX, XLSX, and CSV email exports enter as raw evidence rather than polished product data.

Inputs: OneDrive, Outlook CSV exports
02 Ingestion
Documents become structured text.

ingest_onedrive.py and parse_emails.ps1 walk folders, match matters, and write usable derivatives.

Scripts: ingest_onedrive.py, parse_emails.ps1
03 Processing
Dupes disappear and formats settle down.

email_consolidator.mjs, email_to_md.py, docx_to_md.py, and email_defuzz.mjs build a clean working corpus.

Scripts: email_consolidator.mjs, email_to_md.py, docx_to_md.py, email_defuzz.mjs
04 Sync
Notion becomes the case registry.

notion_wire_cases.py, notion_wire_batch.py, and notion_case_dates.py keep matters, dates, and relations aligned.

Scripts: notion_wire_cases.py, notion_wire_batch.py, notion_case_dates.py
05 AI tagging
Models label the content and fill the gaps.

email_deep_tag.mjs, legal_matters_fill.mjs, and legal_hold_backfill.mjs turn raw material into decision-ready structure.

Scripts: email_deep_tag.mjs, legal_matters_fill.mjs, legal_hold_backfill.mjs
06 QC
Every push gets checked against the corpus.

qc_sweep.mjs, repo_sweep.py, verify_repo_consistency.py, and tactical_brief.py keep the front door honest.

Scripts: qc_sweep.mjs, repo_sweep.py, verify_repo_consistency.py, tactical_brief.py

What this repo demonstrates.

The showcase is public enough to explain the machine, but not permissive enough to pretend this is generic deployware. The scripts are the product. The value is the implementation discipline that wires them together.

Real-time email corpus Inbound and outbound mail matched to case matters in Notion, with deduplication across import batches.
AI document tagging Claude and OpenAI classify filings, transcripts, and correspondence while flagging missing properties.
Case date management Date of loss, complaint filed, discovery cutoff, deposition dates, reserve, and incurred synced into the live registry.
Legal hold tracking Per-matter hold status with backfill logic and auditability instead of spreadsheet folklore.
