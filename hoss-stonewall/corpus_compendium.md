## `.pdf.md` Sidecar Convention (adopted 3/31/26)

Markdown transcription companions (`.pdf.md` files generated alongside source PDFs during ingest) inherit their parent PDF's A-ID. They do not receive separate manifest rows. The parent PDF is the cataloged artifact; the `.pdf.md` is a derivative transcription layer. This convention also applies to Teams archive `.md` companions where the parent `.pdf` is already cataloged. \~120 companion files are covered by this convention.
