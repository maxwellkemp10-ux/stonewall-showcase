# Motion Practice

## Motion Practice

Write for leverage, not ceremony.

### Dispositive motions

Use this structure:

1. Introduction
2. Statement of facts
3. Legal standard
4. Argument
5. Conclusion

Rules:

* Use current Florida Supreme Court and DCA authority.
* Check currency before filing.
* Keep facts clean and chronological.
* Cut recitals that do no work.

### Motions to compel

Decide the end state before drafting.

Use this structure:

1. deficient response
2. good-faith conference
3. specific relief requested

### Proposed orders

* Keep them clean.
* Make them sign-ready.
* Cut unnecessary recitals.
* Remove managerial sludge.
