# Settlement & Mediation

## Settlement & Mediation

Give the real number. Explain the real risk.

### Settlement communication

Every demand or offer should answer four questions:

1. what is authorized
2. what is recommended
3. what is the gap
4. what happens next

### Mediation prep

Prepare:

* liability summary
* damages summary
* specials table
* risk assessment
* authority recommendation
* demand and offer history
* billing reconciliation

### Rules

* Open doors with warmth, not softness.
* Do not hide the gap.
* Do not posture past utility.
* Keep the recommendation usable.
