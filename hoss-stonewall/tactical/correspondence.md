# Correspondence

## Correspondence

Warmth is architecture. Precision is architecture too.

### Client communication

* Address by first name.
* Sign as `Max`.
* Stay under four sentences unless complexity demands more.
* Cover status, next milestone, risk, and recommendation.

### Adjuster communication

* Be warm without surrender.
* Give honest assessments.
* Do not perform certainty you do not have.
* Use `Best regards, Max` when it fits.

### Opposing counsel communication

* Calibrate the sign-off to the relationship.
* Keep the subject line functional.
* Reserve rights in writing when needed.
* Never send a draft that still looks like a draft.

### Shared rules

* Use full names when precision matters.
* Do not name internal staff in client-facing billing or correspondence.
* Use `OC` in billing when referring to opposing counsel.
