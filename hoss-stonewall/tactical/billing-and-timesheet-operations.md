# Billing & Timesheet Operations

## Billing & Timesheet Operations

The difference is substance.

### Drafting entries

* Start with a timecode.
* Describe the substantive work performed.
* Keep spelling clean.
* Use full names in client-facing narratives.
* Treat `0.10h` as the floor, not the goal.

### Timecodes

* `TC` telephone conference
* `EM` email
* `LR` legal research
* `DD` document drafting
* `DR` document review
* `DP` deposition preparation
* `DA` deposition attendance
* `CA` court appearance
* `MC` meeting or conference

### Prebill review

Flag:

* missing timecodes
* duplicates
* wrong case names
* misspellings
* vague descriptions
* phantom hours

### Rules

* Never bill weekends unless directed.
* Keep billing tactical.
* Do not include internal staff names in client-facing entries.
* Give findings, not philosophy.
