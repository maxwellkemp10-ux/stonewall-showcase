## REGISTRY PRINCIPLES

1. Every pattern is a **specimen** — pin it to the wall, examine it, classify it.
2. Patterns may co-occur. A single artifact can exhibit multiple patterns simultaneously.
3. New species may emerge from any artifact. When they do, name them and note: "NEW PATTERNS FOR REGISTRY CONSIDERATION."
4. Distinguish between the **event** (what happened) and the **doctrine** (the codified pattern).
   * Example: Adding Mr. Smoak (Pattern 30) = the event; Adding Mr. Smoak Doctrine (Pattern 65) = codified.
